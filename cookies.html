Cookie policy.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies and similar browser storage help websites remember preferences, maintain essential functions, and - where a visitor agrees - understand how a website is used.

Storage this website uses

  • Essential: required for accessibility, form behaviour, security, and remembering your cookie-notice choice.
  • Analytical: optional measurement tools that would help KDH understand aggregate site use. These must not run before consent.
  • Functional: optional settings that may remember enhanced preferences.

Your control

You may accept optional cookies or choose essential storage only in the website notice. You can also delete or block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking essential storage may affect some site functions.

Current implementation

This website currently stores only the visitor's cookie-preference choice in local browser storage. No analytics or advertising trackers are loaded. If analytics are added later, this policy and the consent controls should be updated before activation.
