Cookie policy.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. They help websites remember preferences, maintain essential functions, and—where you agree—understand how visitors use a site.

Cookies we use

  • Essential: required for security, accessibility, form behavior, and remembering your cookie choice.
  • Analytical: optional measurement tools that help us understand aggregate site use. These should run only after consent.
  • Functional: optional settings that remember enhanced preferences.

Your control

You can accept optional cookies or choose essential cookies only in the website notice. You can also delete or block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking essential storage may affect some site functions.

Current implementation

This website currently stores only the visitor’s cookie-preference choice in local browser storage. If analytics are added, this policy and the consent controls should be updated before activation.
