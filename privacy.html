Legal & compliance

Privacy notice.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

Our commitment

Ken, Daniel & Henry Advocates processes personal data in accordance with Kenya’s Data Protection Act, 2019 and applicable regulations. We collect only information necessary to respond to enquiries, provide legal services, meet professional obligations, and operate this website.

Information we process

This may include contact details, organisation and role information, enquiry content, client matter information, billing records, and limited technical website data.

Purpose and lawful basis

  • Responding to requests and taking steps toward engagement.
  • Performing legal-service agreements.
  • Meeting legal, regulatory, and professional duties.
  • Protecting our legitimate interests in secure business operations.
  • Using consent where required, including optional analytics.

Retention

General enquiries are normally retained for up to 24 months. Client and matter records may be retained for at least seven years after closure, or longer where professional, tax, legal, or limitation requirements apply.

Cross-border transfers

Where service providers process data outside Kenya, KDH applies appropriate safeguards, contractual controls, access restrictions, and transfer assessments consistent with Kenyan law.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, restriction, objection, portability, or deletion where applicable, and may withdraw consent without affecting earlier lawful processing.

Data Protection Officer

Contact the Data Protection Officer at dpo@kdhlaw.co.ke, Nairobi, Kenya. You may also lodge a complaint with Kenya’s Office of the Data Protection Commissioner.
