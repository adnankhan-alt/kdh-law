Our commitment

KDH Advocates LLP processes personal data in accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019 and applicable regulations. We collect only the information necessary to respond to enquiries, provide legal services, meet professional obligations, and operate this website.

Information we process

This may include contact details, organisation and role information, enquiry content, client-matter information, billing records, and limited technical website data.

Purpose and lawful basis

  • Responding to requests and taking steps towards a professional engagement.
  • Performing legal-service agreements.
  • Meeting legal, regulatory, and professional duties.
  • Protecting legitimate interests in secure business operations.
  • Using consent where required, including for any optional analytics.

Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected and to meet applicable legal, regulatory, professional, tax, and limitation requirements.

Cross-border transfers

Where service providers process information outside Kenya, KDH applies appropriate safeguards, contractual controls, access restrictions, and transfer assessments consistent with Kenyan law.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, restriction, objection, portability, or deletion where applicable, and may withdraw consent without affecting earlier lawful processing.

Contact

Privacy questions may be directed to law@kdhadvocates.com, KDH Advocates LLP, IPS Building, 1st Floor, Kimathi Street, Nairobi. You may also lodge a complaint with Kenya's Office of the Data Protection Commissioner.
